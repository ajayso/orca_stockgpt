Large Language Models (LLMs) are game-changers. They hold promise from streamlining information management to enabling cutting-edge AI-driven decisions in businesses. But, it's not all smooth sailing. The concerns of trustworthiness, interpretability, and the very essence of making LLMs business-ready while staying regulatory-compliant are real challenges.
Add to this a notable gap in understanding about the real costs, intricacies, and expertise required for LLMs. This void often leads businesses down paths filled with unexpected hurdles.
